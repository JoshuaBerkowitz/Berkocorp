About Us

Berkocorp is the investment office of the Berkowitz family, with roots in Vancouver and operations in Toronto.

Originating from a real estate business established in the 1950s, Berkocorp now focuses on private equity and venture capital in North America, while continuing to manage legacy real estate holdings.

  • Family owned and operated, with principal-led decision making.
  • Patient, long-term investors with a multi-generational perspective.
  • Concentrated in private markets, with a North America focus.
